Civil Engineer

I graduated from Polytechnique Montréal as a civil engineer in 2016 and have since then gained professional experience as a BIM (Building Information Modeling) Coordinator at a multinational railway company called SYSTRA and as a Project Officer in the transportation field at the AFD (French Development Agency).

I have worked on major overseas transportation projects such as on MyHSR (High Speed Railway between Kuala Lumpur and Singapore) and on HS2 (High Speed Railway between London and Birmingham) for the SYSTRA Group, initially in Paris and then in New Delhi. During my time in India, I also gained experience managing the production of a 3D model video for the VIA HFR Project (High Frequency Rail linking Quebec City and Toronto). Afterwards, I had the opportunity to work for the AFD where I was handling the operational management of the transport portfolio in Indonesia. In addition to monitoring already existing projects, I was able to participate in the identification and implementation of a new project that was aiming to develop a Sustainable Urban Mobility Plan for the metropolitan area of Medan.

My studies and my interest in civil engineering have brought me a wide range of knowledge in the structural field, in soil mechanics, in hydraulics as well as in the field of transportation. As a coordinator I was privileged to work with people from around the world with different specialties, collaborating with both the contracting authority and the consultancy firm. I have improved my knowledge in project preparation and the bidding process. In addition, I developed competence in project coordination using BIM technology.
